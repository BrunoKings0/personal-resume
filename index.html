About me

Bruno Vieira

Hello world! I'm Bruno, born and raised in Brazil, but currently living in Spain in the beautiful and sunny city of Seville. When I'm not working I like to be on the nature, read, write or dedicating some time to my new passion: photography.

I have always been passionate about creation, of any kind, and I decided to study control, automation and robotics engineering to pass my days creating eletronic devices and robots 🤖. During my time at university I discovered that the world of programming and data was much cooler and dynamic, so here I am!

I have been working in the data area for over 8 years and have gone through several moments of paradigm shifts and technologies that this area provides: since the entire ecosystem in on-premises, and then moving on to the cloud working with Data Warehouse, Data Lake, Data Lakehouse and the most recent Data mesh architecture.

My main language is Python, but at a non-professional level I also know some Front-End languages to play web development. I have already worked with all the three main clouds on the market: AWS, Azure and Google. I have also worked with the 2 biggest players in the big data world: Databricks and Snowflake. I have my preferences, but it's a secret haha.
